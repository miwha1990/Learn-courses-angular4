The goal of the DTS Level 1 Certification is to educate personal trainers on how to use the DTS 7 Step System to improve client outcomes by developing a training methodology that is practical and can be used immediately. This course gives you practical skills and maps out exact action steps from assessment to program design.Upon completing this certification you will have practiced, practiced and practiced. The volume of practice ensures that you are completely confident in how to apply the system. Confidence and practical skills are the keys to success in the personal training business.
TOPICS COVERED IN THIS COURSE:
  • Engaging the client from the initial assessment, right through a full year of training
  • How to identify movement imbalances
  • How to prioritize training to address these imbalances
  • The DTS 7 Step System
  • Advanced mobility techniques
  • How to coach exercise form to improve movement
  • Exercise coaching and cueing techniques
  • Practical program design basics
  • Common program design errors
  • Program writing; trainers will write 6-8 programs
  • Program templates
  • DTS FLPS® method
  • How to design a fat loss program
DTS LEVEL 1 CERTIFICATION REQUIREMENTS
To earn the DTS Level 1 Certification, you must successfully complete all components of the DTS Level 1 program: DTS-Level 1 – Certification Course and the DTS-Level 1 in-class Exam. All students will be required to complete the DTS Level 1 exam in-class and achieve a score of 85% to pass. Upon completion of the exam, you will be issued a DTS Level 1 Course Certificate on-site.
